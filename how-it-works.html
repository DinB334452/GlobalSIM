How It Works

  1. Register: Create an account with your email and password.
  2. Fund Wallet: Transfer funds using bank transfer.
  3. Choose Number: Pick a number from our list of countries.
  4. Upload Proof: Upload your payment proof.
  5. Receive Number: Get your number delivered to your dashboard.
